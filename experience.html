Clubs

Computer Science and Engineering Society: TriDev

In Spring of 2022, a group of students and I developed a discord bot that acted as a stock exchange game. Using discord commands, you can check the price of stocks, buy and sell stocks, view the leaderboard to see who has the most money, and view your own portfolio of stocks. We used the discord.js library to create the discord bot in Node, and we used firebase for our database. Also, we used the Finnhub Stock API to fetch live stock quotes.
